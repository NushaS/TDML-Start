We look through photo books to remember the views we saw, write diary entries to reminisce the good memories, and retrace old steps to relive those core memories. But what if you could do all that that on one platform?

"A Trip Down Memory Lane" is the video game Hero's Path brought to your life, where your footsteps are forever imprinted on this virtual globe. At any footstep taken, you have the option to attach a picture, a video, a written entry of what you encountered at that very spot. And whenever you're feeling particularly nostalgic, you can press replay to re-watch all the memories, places, and people you've met on your life journey. There is also an option to share your journey with friends and family, and bond over memories together.
